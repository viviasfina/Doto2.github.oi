Day Night Cycle
The map of Dota experiences a day/night cycle. Totaling 8 minutes, day and night last 4 minutes of real-life time. A sun/moon dial keeps track of day and night in the top of the ingame user interface. Typically heroes see farther through fog of war during the day than night, with only 5 exceptions. Night Stalker has greater night vision, Luna has equal night and day vision with her Lunar Blessing ability, Slark has equal night and day vision of 1800. Night is often a signal for a team to commence ganking. Night Stalker's ultimate Darkness, summons instant darkness and extends the duration of night. The Day/Night Cycle supports a Nightstalker O'Clock icon for Darkness.
High Ground
Without a ward or allied unit on the high ground, a hero cannot see from the low ground up onto the high ground. On the low ground a ranged unit attacking up the high ground has a 25% miss chance on attacks. This mechanic is especially significant in the middle lane river and the entrances into the bases. It also helps a defending team repel aggression onto their tier 3 towers.
Unique Attack Modifiers
With certain Items and skills, Unique Attack Modifiers, or Orb Effects, give Hero attacks a variety of effects. It is important to know these effects do not stack and only one type of buff can be added per attack. Examples include Outworld Destroyer's damaging Arcane Orb, Bash from Skull Basher, and the slow effect from Eye of Skadi. Some skills from heroes give attack modifiers but do not qualify as Unique Attack Modifiers.
